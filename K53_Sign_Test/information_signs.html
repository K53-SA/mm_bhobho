• Name: Co-ordinated traffic signals sign

• Where: Informs you of the traffic signals on this route that they are co-ordinated and that the signals will be green at all the subsequent intersections if you maintain the indicated speed.

• Action: Maintain the same recommended speed indicated. However, make sure that it is safe to enter every intersection and look out for drivers that do not yield.

• Name: Multi-phase traffic signals sign

• Where: The traffic lights do not change simply from red to green, but have more phases to allow vehicles to turn against oncoming traffic.

• Purpose: It points out that the signals at this intersection will not follow the normal sequence.

• Action: Never move off when approaching traffic moves off. Wait until the signal is green. The sequence of the traffic signals at this intersection can be different times.
