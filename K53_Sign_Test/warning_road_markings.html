• Name: Continuity line.

• Where: Located in any road.

• Purpose: Cautions you that there is a break in the continuing part of the roadway and to inform you that if you are in a lane that has a line on one side, that lane will soon turn off from the road which you are now travelling.

• Action: Yield to traffic that wants to change lanes. Check you blind spot to make sure there are no other vehicles that might cut infront. The continuity lines will soon change to a painted island or channelizing line, which will make it illegal to change direction.

• Where: Located in any road with two-way traffic.

• Purpose: Cautions you that vehicles on the other side of the road marking are moving in the other direction.
