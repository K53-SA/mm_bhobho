• Name: Traffic circle ahead (mini-circle or roundabout) sign

• Where: Located on any public road.

• Purpose: Warns you of a traffic circle ahead where you may need to yield to other traffic.

• Action: Safely slow down and stop or drive around the circle at a safe speed. Be on a lookout for road traffic signs and markings which will let you know whether you have right of way or whether you have to yield.

12 of 18

• Name: Two-way traffic sign

• Where: Located at urban areas

• Purpose: Warns that a one-way street you are on will soon be crossed by a street that carries two-way traffic.

• Action: When driving on the left hand lane and want to turn to the right at the first intersection, safely change to the right hand lane in good time. When driving on the right hand lane and want to turn to the left at the first intersection, safely change to the left hand lane in good time.
