• Name: Railway crossing sign

• Where: Road near a railway line

• Purpose: It indicates that there is a railway crossing with one track ahead.

• Action: Carefully slow down and be on the lookout for a train, if there is a train approaching, stop. When there is no stop line or sign the driver should stop 3 metres away from the nearest track, not on the track or in the railway reserve.
