• Name: Woonerf

• Where: Any public road

• Purpose: To forbid all vehicles with a mass exceeding 3500 kg, or vehicles which have more than 10 seats, except for local access or delivery to enter the area. Pedestrians have a right of way in the street. Vehicles driving through the area will be breaking their journey when passing by. It limits the speed of the vehicles passing through the area to 30 km/h.

• Action: Drive carefully and obey the rules and be on a look out for children playing on the road.
