• Name: Traffic movement affected by obstruction

• Where: Any road.

• Purpose: Indicates to you by means of a diagram which shows you that one of the lanes of a carriageway comes to an end.

• Action: Whenever a lane converges, ends, merges or is added, make sure in time that there is a big enough gap in the traffic to merge with the traffic. You should yield to other traffic, which might merge.

• Name: Engage lower gear

• Where: Displayed on your left-hand side of the road.

• Purpose: To warn of the steep descent and you should engage to a lower gear.

• Action: Heavy motor vehicle drivers should engage to a lower gear so that the vehicle will move down the descent slowly. Engage an even lower gear if the first one did not have an effect to keep the speed constant and protect the brakes against overheating.
