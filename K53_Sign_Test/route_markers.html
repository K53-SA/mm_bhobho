• Name: Advance transport trailblazer

• Where: Any road which is not a freeway and it is located on the left-hand side.

• Purpose: Shows you the direction of a transport terminus from a route that would otherwise have taken you past a bus terminus, harbour, airport or heliport.

• Action: Traffic signs will give you the directions to the terminus and be on a lookout for the transport trailblazer that will follow.
