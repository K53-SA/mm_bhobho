• Name: Surface step sign

• Where: Located in any road.

• Purpose: Warns of the level of the right-hand side of the carriageway is higher than the left-hand side.

• Action: Safely slow down and be aware that your vehicle may tilt to the left if you straddle the step between the higher and lower level.

11 of 17

• Name: Soft shoulder sign

• Where: Located in any road

• Purpose: Warns you of a softer road material on the road than reasonably expected and could be dangerous for anyone who wants to move off the road.

• Action: Never drive or stop on the shoulder. When it is un-avoidable, for avoiding a collision, slow down as much as possible because the vehicle will pull in the direction of the shoulder when you move off the road.

• Name: Loose stones.

• Where: Located in any road.

• Purpose: Warns you of loose stones or gravel on the road.

• Action: Safely slow down and be on a lookout for oncoming vehicles that could throw up stones that could damage your vehicle. Vehicles braking and turning capacity could also be affected negatively.
